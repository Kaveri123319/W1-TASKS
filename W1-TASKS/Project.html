Delicious Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken(cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala powder
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required for cooking rice

Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes. Drain the water before cooking.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish and keep aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Mix in the chicken or mutton pieces, turmeric powder, garam masala, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaf. Cook until the meat is browned.
  5. Add the salt, and a little water, and cook until the meat is tender and the gravy thickens.
  6. In another pot, bring water to a boil and cook the soaked rice until it's 70% cooked. Drain the rice.
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture. Add some water (about 1/4 cup), cover, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint leaves. Serve hot with raita or salad.
